Monitoring

Easy or complete monitoring and control

Control the work of rigs through both the web interface and the mobile application. You can access hash rates, errors, power consumption, and much more regardless of your location. The program will track errors and overload the miner or the whole rig. You can also set access rights to allow normal monitoring of or full access to your rig.

Installing
the system

Simply burn the image to a disk or USB flash drive and connect to the rig, and the system will automatically detect your hardware, apply settings, and display the settings on the control panel. Setting up firmware and overclocking cards will not be difficult, because you’ll have access to examples of settings for thousands of different maps. Additionally, RaveOS customer support will always be available on social networks and through instant messenger.
